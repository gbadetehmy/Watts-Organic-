At Watts Organic Farm, we believe that healthy soil grows healthy food. Our 50-acre family farm uses regenerative agricultural practices that restore biodiversity and sequester carbon.

  • 100% Certified Organic (No synthetic pesticides)
  • Water conservation through drip irrigation
  • Solar-powered greenhouses and facilities
